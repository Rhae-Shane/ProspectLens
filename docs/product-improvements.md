# Product Improvements

## 5 Weaknesses

1. No CRM integration
2. Single-user, no auth
3. Research quality varies by company size
4. No collaborative editing
5. Static report format

## Top 3 Improvements

1. CRM Integration
2. Confidence Scoring
3. Team Workspaces

## Buyer, User, Payment

- Buyer: VP Sales / RevOps
- User: AEs and SDRs
- Why they pay: reduces research time from hours to minutes

## Success Metrics

- Time to briefing under 3 minutes
- 60% weekly active reps
- 80% reports opened before meetings

## 4-Week AI Roadmap

Week 1: Confidence scoring. Week 2: CRM MVP. Week 3: Templates. Week 4: Battlecards.

## Risks

Cost scaling, task queue needed, LLM outage risk.

## Feature to Remove

Generic follow-up chat.

## Feature to Add

Meeting Outcome Capture.

## 90-Day Roadmap

Month 1: Design partners. Month 2: Team workspaces. Month 3: Enterprise SSO.

## First Change

Add confidence scoring to every report section.
